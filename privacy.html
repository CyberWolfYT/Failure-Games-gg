Privacy Policy — Failure Games

Last updated: 2026

Failure Games (the "App"), published by CYBERWOLF, is an offline single-player game arcade. We respect your privacy.

Data we collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It does not use accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking, and it makes no network requests for your data. Your game progress and high scores are saved only on your own device (in local browser/app storage) and never leave it.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Permissions

The App requests no special device permissions.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact
